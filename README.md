Facility Cost Estimator
A Progressive Web App (PWA) for preparing "Class 4" (AACE) cost estimates for industrial facilities ranging from 50 to 500 million euros. It runs entirely in the browser, works offline, and can be installed on mobile devices or desktops.

Features
Industrial Scope: Pre-loaded with 8 major disciplines (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Piping, Structural, Instrumentation, HVAC, Fire Protection) and realistic unit prices for large-scale facilities.
Editable Data: Every cell (description, unit, quantity, price) is editable. Changes are saved automatically to your browser's local storage.
Import/Export:
Save Data: Download your current estimate as a .json file to back up or share your work.
Load Data: Import a previously saved .json file to continue working.
Export Report: Generate a clean, printable HTML report with one click.
Mobile First: Designed specifically for phones. Large touch targets, collapsible sections, and a responsive layout.
Installable (PWA): Add it to your Android home screen for a native app experience without the app store.
Offline Support: Once loaded, the app works without an internet connection.
